FAQ

  • What platforms do you work with?
    We adapt to your comfort level. Our favorite tools include Notion, Airtable, Tally, Paperform, Zapier, Circle, and no-code/low-code AI apps like Make and Softr. We help you choose systems that feel good — not just those with the most features. You won’t need to be “techy” to use what we set up.
  • Do you only work with wellness businesses?
    Primarily, yes — but we’re open to other heart-led, purpose-driven founders. If your brand prioritizes emotional resonance, transformation, or sustainable growth, we’re probably a great fit. We believe values alignment is more important than industry.
  • I’m overwhelmed with tools. Can you help me simplify?
    Yes. We specialize in system decluttering. Whether you’re juggling six platforms or don’t know where to start, we’ll help you eliminate friction, streamline what matters, and create a single intuitive flow that supports you, not drains you.
  • Will I be able to manage these systems after setup?
    Absolutely. Every setup comes with a visual map, video walkthroughs, and support hours (or async Q&A). Our goal is to leave you with not only working systems, but clarity and confidence. If needed, we’ll also train your VA or team.
  • What makes your approach different from typical automation experts?
    We blend strategy, emotion, and design — not just systems. While most automation feels cold or overly complex, we prioritize user experience, story-based flows, and intuitive logic. Our setups feel like extensions of your brand — gentle, efficient, and human-centered.
  • Do you offer done-for-you or coaching-style services?
    Both. You can choose between strategic consulting (we co-create your roadmap), done-for-you builds (we handle setup and visuals), or hybrid formats. If you prefer to stay hands-on with guidance, we offer that too — tailored to your capacity and pace.
